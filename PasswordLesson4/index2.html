Password Lesson 4: Multi-Factor Authentication

In this challenge, you will have to select the second factor which seems to be the most appropriate for the given scenario

Sally is visiting her friend for Thanksgiving during COVID-19 and might need to quickly check her email because she has an important report waiting for her the next day. Sally does not like taking her laptop along and her phone is not a smartphone. Which of the following would be the safest second factor authentication for her to use in this case?

Call/ SMS on Sally's phone

Sally's location

A hardware token
