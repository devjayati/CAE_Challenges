Password Lesson 1: Whack-a-Number!

In the following challenge, you will be playing the Whack-a-Mole game, but with a twist! You will have to decide what the 6-digit pattern is for the mole that peeps from the hole. If you think that there is no pattern to it, just type in "RANDOM". If you are correct, a 4-digit numeric flag will be displayed.
